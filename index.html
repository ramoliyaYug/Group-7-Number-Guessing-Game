Rules of the Game

  • Guess a number within the given range.
  • Follow hints like "Too High" or "Too Low" to refine your guesses.
  • Keep guessing until you correctly identify the number!

How to Play

  • Step 1: Start the game and choose your range.
  • Step 2: Enter your guesses in the input field.
  • Step 3: Use the feedback provided to narrow down your guesses.

Rewards

  • Earn points for each correct guess.
  • Get bonus points for consecutive wins.
