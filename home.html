About Game

Easy

  • Guess a number between 1 and 100. Suitable for beginners!

Medium

  • Guess a number between 1 to 500. A Little bit challenging!

Hard

  • Guess a number between 1 to 1000. The most challenging!

How to play

  • Step 1: Guess the number within the range.
  • Step 2: Follow hints like "Too High" or "Too Low".
  • Step 3: Keep guessing until you win!

Reward

  • Earn points for each correct guess.
  • Get bonus points for consecutive wins.
